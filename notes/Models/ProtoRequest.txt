====== ProtoRequest ======
Created Saturday 04 February 2017

An imitation of a real Request record.  Each [[ProtoRequest]] attached to a [[ProtoEvent]] will cause a real [[Request]] to be generated when the [[ProtoEvent]] is used to generate a real Event.
